Settings

Darkroom never stores your key on a server. It stays in this browser and is sent only through our proxy to the provider you choose.

Keys are validated with a lightweight request to the provider before saving. Darkroom's backend proxy forwards each request statelessly — it does not log, cache, or persist your key at any point.
